About

Known by the name Cricketer Yoga during his high school times, Yoga has always been obsessed with fitness and fashion products. He has always inspired people to follow his path towards being fit and fashionable. His life took a turn when he was selected as a finalist for Mister Glam India from Tamil Nadu. It motivated him to expand his audience and to begin a fashion vlog to provide free advice for fashion and fitness.
Yoga also invites women to follow his vlog as he recommends products on women too.
